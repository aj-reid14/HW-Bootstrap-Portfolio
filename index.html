About Me

Who is Andre Reid? Some call him "The One With the Round Frame". Others refer to him as "The IT Guy". Legends tell tales of him roaming the hallways of University of Miami Hospital, fixing all computer problems in sight. Recently, he has been spotted in the realm known as Allen Hall, allegedly growing his skills in the world of web development. In recent years, there have even been reports of him walking the campus at Full Sail University in Orlando, leaving with the title of Valedictorian.

Who is Andre Reid, many have their own perspective, but he is simply a man on a mission.
